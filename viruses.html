Viruses

Viruses are harmful programs that can infect your computer or device. They spread quickly and can damage your files, steal information, or make your device stop working properly.

How Viruses Work

  • Infection: Viruses often spread through email attachments, downloads, or infected websites.
  • Damage: Once inside your device, they can delete files, slow down performance, or steal personal information.
  • Spreading: Viruses can spread to other devices through infected files or links.

Types of Viruses

  • File Viruses: Infect files and programs on your computer.
  • Macro Viruses: Target programs like Word or Excel.
  • Worms: Self-replicating viruses that spread without needing a host file.

How to Protect Your Device

  • Use Antivirus Software: Install and regularly update antivirus programs to detect and remove viruses.
  • Don’t Open Unknown Links: Be careful with links or attachments from unknown senders.
  • Update Software: Keep your operating system and applications updated to fix security holes.

What to Do if Infected

  • Run a Virus Scan: Use antivirus software to scan your device for viruses.
  • Remove the Virus: Follow the antivirus program's instructions to remove any detected viruses.
  • Restore Files: If files are lost or damaged, restore them from a backup if available.
